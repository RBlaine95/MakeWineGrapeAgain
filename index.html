What is Make Wine Grape Again (MWGA)?

Make Wine Grape Again is an easy to use Wine Management System designed for small Wine Cellars. We were contacted by Rocco de Villiers of Cape Crush do build a system for him to use.

MWGA allows you to track batches of wine, starting with raw grapes from suppliers all the way to bottling and selling.

Features include:

  • Blending up to 10 wines together
  • Balling and Temperature graph
  • Tracking what chemicals were added to each batch
  • Cost calculator
  • Creating sub-batches of batches
  • Keeping record of grape origins and supplier
